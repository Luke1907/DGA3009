About Me

Hi, my name is Luke and I am a multidisciplinary designer; my work ranges from branding, packaging design and editorial design to UX/UI design. I am currently in my final year reading for a degree in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. My dissertation explores the growing concept of ‘the brand experience.’ Through my practice-led research, my aim is to develop a contemporary brand and rethink the traditional retail concept of the local horticulture industry.
